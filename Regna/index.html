New Boy Cheat Sheet

Learn different ways to get around the rules that are not in the standard

Get Started

Basic Rules

There are many basic rules that you should learn quickly if you want to stay safe at this school. No.1 If you have any sort of contraband do not tell anyone you have it. No.2 get to know your RFO. No.3 Get to know your leadership and know your friends.

Rule No.1

So your are a new boy at the school and you have brought some items that you are not supposed to have. The first rule is, if you have any items that could potentially get you into trouble, do not tell anyone. This is because in case you have a falling out with this person they have leverage against you that you do not want. Also it means that they could rat you out if they are ever in trouble

Rule No.2

This rule can be just as important as any of the other ones. This is because if you are ever in a bad situation but your are on good terms with your RFO the severity of your punishment can go way down. Also getting to know your RFO is something that you ahould just do because that can help you out in times that you least expect it.

Fun Fact

Kid at this school will rat you out if it means that they stay safe. So just think about that when you want to share sensative information with someone.

Call To Action

Rule No.3

Getting to know your leadership and your friends are one of the most important things that you can do here. You need to be able to know which members of the leadership you can trust and which ones you can not. This is important because you need to think about your future here. You should start making friends that are good kids and do well so when you go into higher grades you have friends that are in high positions.

Overview

If you follow these rules you should be able to get through the school year with no problems. These rules will garantee that you never have to be in the deputy commondant or commandants office and facing expulsion or suspension.
